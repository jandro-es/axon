# Templates

Note templates with consistent YAML frontmatter, used by Obsidian (and by AXON
when it authors notes). Provided: Daily Note, Atomic Note, Project, Knowledge
Source, MOC.

Keep frontmatter keys small and stable — AXON reads `type`, `status`, `tags`,
`created`/`updated` and the source-only fields, and must never reorder or strip
keys it doesn't recognise.
